What is ActiveTrack really?

ActiveTrack is a user-friendly fitness tracking application that helps you monitor and analyze the calories. Whether you're running outdoors or exercising indoors, ActiveTrack makes it easy to log your training sessions, track your progress and visualize your results through a line chart!

Using ActiveTrack is simple and straightforward:

  • Log your workouts: Just navigate a bit further down and use the form to start adding your training records. Fill in details like the type of activity, calories burned, training type (indoor or outdoor), the training time and a short description of the workout.
  • View your progress: Immediately after adding a training record it will show you how many training records you have added so far including an average calories burn. In the past data section you can filter your data by the type of training and see the results in a line chart, helping you track your performance over the past 7 days.
  • Analyze your data: The line chart visually represents your calories burned, making it easy to spot trends and improvements.
  • Stay motivated: By keeping track of your progress, you can push yourself to achieve new fitness milestones and stay consistent in your workouts!
