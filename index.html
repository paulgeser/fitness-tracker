What is ActiveTrack really?

ActiveTrack is a user-friendly fitness tracking application that helps you monitor and analyze the calories you burn during different types of physical activities. Whether you're running outdoors or exercising indoors, ActiveTrack makes it easy to log your training sessions, track your progress, and visualize your results through a line chart!

Using ActiveTrack is simple and straightforward:

  • Log your workouts: Start by adding your training records. Fill in details like the type of activity, calories burned, training type (indoor or outdoor), and a short description of the workout.
  • View your progress: Check your records to see your burned calories over time. You can filter your data by the type of training and see the results in a line chart, helping you track your performance over the past 7 days.
  • Analyze your data: The line chart visually represents your calories burned, making it easy to spot trends and improvements.
  • Stay motivated: By keeping track of your progress, you can push yourself to achieve new fitness milestones and stay consistent in your workouts!
